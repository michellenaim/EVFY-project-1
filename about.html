About EVFY.

EVFY – a cutting-edge clean tech start-up – offers green solutions, starting with electric mobility, to help communities live more sustainable lives.

With EVFY, consumers become members of our sustainability program, known as The Sustainability Index.This program provides substantial savings and rewards for being a conscious consumer aiming to live carbon free.

Through our program, EVFY aims to help people become as carbon-free as possible. We educate and guide on lifestyle changes and provide services and resources to 'go green'.

The EVFY Sustainability Index is the new benchmark for consumers to understand the environmental impact of their decisions and social capital to share amongst peers for a broader goal of making a world-wide impact.

Our Mission

To empower people to reduce their carbon footprint in an effortless and sustainable way.

The EVFY Team

Collaboration is one of the keys for unlocking sustainability, with leaders from all sectors of society agreeing that solving environmental and social challenges requires unparalleled cooperation. At EVFY, we are focused on solving the world’s most pressing issues facing climate change today.

We cannot be hesitant or half-hearted about living sustainably. Our team culture is to drive innovation, and continuous engagement by being open, transparent, ethical and responsible in order to find solutions for how people can live the most sustainable lives possible.

The Sustainability Index.

The Sustainability Index

The Sustainability Index is a tiering system to reward strong sustainable behavior. It is an opportunity to earn points based on choices that consumers make in their everyday lives.

The program provides consumers with a sustainability score based on daily consumption choices made around reducing their carbon footprint.

The EVFY Team

Our approach is three-fold. To improve your sustainability score, EVFY helps you to:

  • Incorporate daily energy usage within your home and by using your electric vehicle.
  • Reduce your energy bill and enjoy substantial savings.
  • Implement a fully sustainable lifestyle via a phased rollout of ways to improve consumption behaviors with discounted products and services including food, clothing, air travel, hotels and other elements to help improve your sustainability index - and ultimately live as carbon free as possible.
